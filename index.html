ONNX Operators
• An Operator represent a fundamental operation on the tensor in DAG
• Wide spectrum of complexity Add , Min , Max , ArgMin , MaxPool , BatchNormalization , Conv , LeakyRelu
,LSTM ….
• ONNX does not provide any implementation of the standardized ops
• Ops belong to a domain name ai.onnx , ai.onnx.ml or a custom domain name
for custom ops
